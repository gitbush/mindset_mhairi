What actually is transformation coaching?

Awesome question! In short, Transformation coaching helps you make profound changes to ultimately live happy and fulfilled. Together we take an inside out approach. So unlike traditional life coaching which centres around goals and external changes for surface level shifts, we go a little deeper….but not moonlight meditation deep, don’t worry. Through our work I help you find and harness your innate power and resilience (whether you believe it’s there or not). Following a process of unlocking blockers and mindful self-awareness you step beyond the psychology that’s been holding you back from living a life you love. .

How my program works:

Step one

Discovery call

This is one of my favourite parts! We do an intensive deep dive into all things you. We look at what’s important to you, what’s working for you in your life, what’s working against you and start to pull out common themes that we can explore in the coaching sessions.

Step two

DISC

Depending on the program you choose we may do a DISC profiling session. This is an incredibly powerful tool, pioneered by Dr Marston of Harvard University, to provide a deeper understanding of self. Following completion of your online questionnaire we pull out your fears, motivators and drivers leaving you with the knowledge to improve your communication, empower your relationships and drive your own performance more effectively.

Step three

Bi-weekly coaching

Then the fun really begins. Our coaching sessions will be structured in a way that becomes familiar and easy but also centred around you. We work with your conscious and subconscious to unlock your specific barriers. Through powerful questioning I act as your guide to lead you back to your innate power. I then help you tap into it to elevate your life.
