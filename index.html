Hey, I'm Mhairi

Transformation coach for talented women who want more from life.

I worked for many years in senior roles with fancy job titles and salaries, but I was never truly fulfilled.

After completing a Medical Biology degree I knew my passion lay out with the lab. But what to do? Youthful ignorance firmly in hand I gravitated to where I thought I ‘should’. I was led by society’s depiction of success. When I got there I realised I’d been so busy becoming what I thought I was supposed to that I’d completely lost touch with what I actually wanted.

What changed my world? Complete disaster. It was the best thing that happened to me. Read more about my journey to happiness on my terms and how I can help you with yours.
