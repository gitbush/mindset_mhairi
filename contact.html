The difference between your main 3 offerings?

How difficult do you find the challenges you are bringing to transformation coaching? The main differences are length of program, frequency of sessions and depth of discovery therefore change. Swift = short, frequent and broad. Signature = medium, every 2-3 weeks and extensive. Shift = Long, every 2-3 weeks and total.

WOULD YOU TELL ME IF COACHING WASN’T RIGHT FOR ME?

Absolutely! I make it a priority to only take on clients for whom coaching is right for and I am a good fit for. That's why the initial Clarity call is so important. Its free, no obligation and within that we explore your needs and our chemistry.
