I WENT FROM OUTWARDLY HAVING IT ALL BUT INWARDLY ANXIOUS & FULL OF SELF DOUBT TO…DIVORCED

IT CHANGED THE GAME AND THAT IS WHERE MY JOURNEY TO SUCCESS TRULY BEGAN

There I was, early 30’s, divorced, in a career which supposedly ticked all the boxes but didn’t tick mine and feeling like total sh&t about myself. I had no self worth and no idea how to change that.

The real work started from within. My unhappiness was nothing to do with the actions I was taking. I’d previously lived by the ‘feel the fear and do it anyway’ mantra which was insanely effective in keeping me progressing, but totally ineffective in living a happy, purposeful, powerful life!

So I did something very different for me. I made the decision to work on me. I invested in me with no outcome expected other than I would undoubtedly be in a better place than I started.

And you know what happened? It only bloody worked! I started to make choices from an authentic place rather than a superficial one. I changed jobs to a company with a heart and purpose more aligned with what I stood for. More importantly I turned down a Directorship and ridiculous salary to make that change. And it felt good!

I started a diploma in life coaching because I supported the ethos. I loved the idea of helping people step beyond their psychology and leap forward into their dream life. I met an incredible partner and we had a beautiful baby boy even though I’d previously been terrified about how having children might affect my ‘success’ in my career…. The positive, life fulfilling, incredible magic stuff just kept coming. All because I had actually spent the time, effort and energy in the one place it seemed most alien to: on myself.

So, that’s a nice story, but how does that help you?
